WHO AM I?

Hi! I’m Meghan, a twenty-year-old Media, Journalism and Publishing Student
at Oxford Brookes University. I’m an aspiring writer hoping to become an author
one day; I’m interested primarily in the romance and thriller genre’s (with a
little bit of mystery thrown in there for some added fun). I’ve always had a
love for books and anything creative and that’s why I was so eager to find an
online space to start sharing my creations.

    WHY A WEBSITE BASED ON TYPOGRAPHY?

    Parts of my website revolves around Print Media which I’m constantly sifting through.
    I think it’s important to keep up to date with the world’s affairs and to do this we
    have to read newspapers, magazines and books. The way they’re presented to us is
    actually really important and I enjoy exploring that concept.
