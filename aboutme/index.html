About Me

Hi! I'm Meghan Tole and I'm a 20 year old Media, Journalism and Publishing student at Oxford Brookes University.
I'm an aspiring writer hoping to become an author one day; interested primarily in the romance and thriller genre's (with a little bit of mystery thrown in there for some added fun).
I really enjoy spending time sifting through different social media networks and keeping up to date with the news.
As a person I think it's important to be aware of the world around us and social media is the easiest way to do just that.
Anything to do with art has always been my passion and that's why I'm so interested in typography, especially in print media.
The reason I chose the course at university was because I'm passionate about going about things the right way and wanted to understand the industry before entering it myself.
